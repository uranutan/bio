  1. (Mobile App) My arXiv: Keep Track of Your arXiv Topics

    First Release: Oct 2020 (research tools)

    Researchers can keep track of who published what on arXiv everyday. Stay informed on trendy topics. A few minutes on the app, you will have the best conversation starter with colleagues. Staying up to date with the literature can never be easier !

    Flutter Dart Xcode/iOS Android

  2. (Web App) Growth Snapshot: Visualise Your Child's Growth Statistics

    First Release: Aug 2020 (parenting tools)

    https://www.growth-snapshot.com/

    Parents can keep a picture snapshot of your child's look, age, height, weight and BMI with respecitve percentiles computed from World Health Organisation (WHO) standard growth model. Suitable for all children of the world.

    Javascript React Node Heroku HTML&CSS

  3. (Genomics Data Challenge) Preterm Birth Prediction: Transcriptomics DREAM Challenge

    Date: June-Aug 2019

    My fanatistic team with Rohan Williams, Eric Hill, Nay Min Saw at Singapore Centre on Environmental Life Sciences Engineering

    We developed an emsembled model using longitudinal gene expression data to predict gestation age of a pregnancy at sampling. See the final report put together by the organisers of the challenge on bioRxiv Crowdsourcing assessment of maternal blood multiomics for predicting gestational age and preterm birth.

    R Python Ensemble of Machine Learning models Cross Validation

  5. (Skin Image Data) Children SkinCast Image Classifications

    Date: Sep 2017 (medical image analysis tool)

    joint work with Dr. Damjan Vukcevic and Murdoch Children Research Institute.

    We developed a texton-clustering model using tools from Computer Vision to learn skin texture patterns from 235 original silicon cast images. This algorithms can be used as a tool to speed up manual grading process by practitioners and flags cases of certain skin conditions.

    R Python/OpenCV Mixture Models Cross Validation
